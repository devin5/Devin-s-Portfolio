ABOUT ME

My name is Devin Dias. I am a Developer located in Austin, TX. I love to code, but I would say my biggest passion in learning new technologies! I believe there is no greater power than knowledge. I understand it is important to be open minded and receptive to others with different backgrounds and experiences!

MY WORK

FRONT-END

I have built websites and single page applications using Figma for design and developing code with HTML5, CSS, LESS/SASS, Javascript, Bootstrap, jQuery, Redux, and React. My favorite front-end technology is by far React. I love its approach to constructing small, interchangeable, and reusable components which decrease development time while increasing productivity and scalability.

Back-END

I have built servers utilizing Node.js and Express with RESTful api's for front-end applications. I have designed and managed databases in SQLite for development and PostgreSQL for production. My favorite libraries are Knex and Node.js! I appreciate the one language for everything paradigm, and I believe it is amazing you can write the front-end application, the API, and even the SQL Queries in Javascript.
